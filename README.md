## 📊 Debt Collection Performance Analysis

---

### 📌 Business Problem

A financial services organization needed to evaluate collections performance across agents, risk segments, and time periods to identify performance gaps and optimization opportunities.

---

### 📂 Dataset Overview

The dataset contains 30 accounts with the following attributes:

* **Agent Name**
* **Amount Assigned**
* **Amount Recovered**
* **Risk Level**
* **Month**
* **Region**
* **Contact Attempts**
* **Account Age**
* **Resolution Status**

---

### 🛠 Tools Used

* **Microsoft Excel** – Data Cleaning, Pivot Tables, Dashboard
* **SQL** – Aggregation & Filtering Practice
* **Power BI** – Data Modeling & Interactive Dashboard

---

### 📈 Key Insights

* John achieved the highest recovery rate at **70%**, while David recorded the lowest at **55.89%**.
* High-risk accounts demonstrated the lowest recovery performance.
* March recorded the strongest overall recovery rate.
* Accounts older than 120 days showed reduced recovery success.

---

### 💡 Recommendations

* Implement coaching for lower-performing agents.
* Adjust strategy for high-risk and aged accounts.
* Replicate March operational strategies in future periods.
